Why We Are Special


Meat and Poultry

We source our grass fed meat only from certified halal exporters in New Zealand. We only use daisy chicken from halal organic farm poultry suppliers. The dairy products comes from halal suppliers in Western Australia and New Zealand.
Marine Products

Our selected fish supplies comes direct from the Fisherman's Jetty in Tanjong Karang to ensure freshness and the best of qualities. The lobsters, prawns and king crabs are air flown daily from Sandakan. You can be rest assured that we only serve premium quality marine products.

Vegetables

The vegetables in our kitchen comes directly from organic farms in Cameron Highland and Kundasang, Sabah. We also get some of our supplies from Australia to get the best of qualities.
